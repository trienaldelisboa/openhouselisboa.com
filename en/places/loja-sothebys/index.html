Description

The interior design brief for these estate agent premises in Parque das Nações was to reinvent the interior space of a pre-existing shop located in an office building. The client required a number of individual offices and conference rooms with support zones, as well as a public zone in which to receive clients. The layout strategy freed up as large a space as possible for the public area, with the double ceiling height adding to the feeling of space and abundance of light. Given the inspiration for the reception area was as a public space, it contrasts with the private, secluded character of the offices and other work spaces, which seem to evoke the idea of being a small building of their own.
