Description

This studio is like a real-life model where the architects also became artisans, experimenting with and testing building techniques using raw natural materials, such as wood, earth and lime. It is a space of reflection and experimentation that has become a prototype for new renovation projects in the city and a laboratory for the continued use of rapidly disappearing building materials and methods, which have been employed here to meet contemporary challenges, such as environmental impact and spatial comfort.
