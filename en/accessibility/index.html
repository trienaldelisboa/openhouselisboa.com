Accessibility

  • Visits for blind and low vision people
  • Visits for deaf people
  • Visits for people with intellectual disabilities

We want to reach everyone. In this edition we offer for the first time a group of accessibility visits to several spaces of the itinerary, with adequate mediation for the following public: blind, low vision, deaf people or with intellectual disabilities. Welcome!

These visits are organised by Locus Acesso with the Lisbon Architecture Triennale, and some of the institutions involved.

Visits for blind and low vision people

These allow the comprehension of the spaces and their functions with the aid to description and tactile experiences. We can meet you at the nearest stop, whether its bus or tube. Please contact us via email locusacesso@gmail.com. The maximum number of people on each visit doesn’t include guests. If you wish to bring someone, the maximum number is 2.
