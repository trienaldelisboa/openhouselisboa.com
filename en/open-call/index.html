Suggest a Space

Do you know of a space that could be added to OH Lisboa? We are open to suggestions that might be part of this edition’s itinerary. So we invite all the OH Lisboa fans, lovers of Lisbon and architects to participate by filling a brief form.

You can be an author with a work under construction or recently finished, or an owner of a surprising space that wants to open its doors to this event. Or even a city flâneur with a special place you always wanted to visit but never could.

Please tell us about your suggestions

Suggestions

Open House Plus

We are looking for independent and self-financed projects to be part of the parallel program of OH Lisboa 2017 events. We are looking for projects on different formats that preferably are related to architecture, landscape architecture or urbanism. From exhibitions, debates or guided tours, the events should happen on the weekend of 23 and 24 of September 2017. The curators will make a selection that will be added to the itinerary, and promoted in several different materials by OH Lisboa.

We look forward to your proposals till July 31st Send them via email to: openhouselisboa@trienaldelisboa.com.
